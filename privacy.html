Privacy Policy

Effective June 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Rehearse ("we," "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our AI interview‑coaching service over text message.

Information We Collect

  • Phone number — to send and receive your messages.
  • Message content — the texts you send and our replies, to provide coaching and improve your experience.
  • Subscription & payment data — handled by Stripe. We do not store your full card details; Stripe processes payments and shares limited information (e.g., subscription status, the phone number you provide) with us.
  • Usage data — basic logs needed to operate and secure the service.

How We Use It

  • To provide the coaching service, generate responses, and send reminders.
  • To process your subscription and prevent abuse.
  • To maintain, secure, and improve the service.

Service Providers We Share With

We share only what's necessary with vendors that help run Rehearse:

  • Sendblue — to deliver and receive your text messages.
  • Groq — to generate AI responses from your messages.
  • Stripe — to process subscription payments.
  • Render — to host the service.

We do not sell your personal information.

Messaging Consent & Opt‑Out

By texting Rehearse you consent to receive automated text messages from us, including reminders. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP; reply HELP for help.

Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as needed to provide the service and meet legal obligations. Conversation context is limited and may be cleared automatically. You can request deletion (see below).

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or to object to certain processing. To exercise these, email us at niah@changeist.org.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, but no system is perfectly secure.

Children

Rehearse is not directed to anyone under 18, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be posted here with a new effective date.

Contact

Questions or requests? Email niah@changeist.org.
